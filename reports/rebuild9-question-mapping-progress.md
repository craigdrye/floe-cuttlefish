## Blocked / Do Not Wire Directly Yet

- Introduction to Modern Statistics / OpenIntro IMS: raw prompts are mostly open-ended exercises, have no answer choices, and sampled solution alignment is unreliable.
- OpenIntro Statistics: open-ended exercises with no choices or clean worked-solution structure.
- NYSED 2024 Grade 8 Math: PDF extraction lost equations, answer choices, and answer keys.
- STAAR released-test extracts: current raw rows generally contain answer-key excerpts but no structured choices/correct answers; they need a better PDF/HTML extractor before import.
- Numbas: large and valuable, but question bodies are parameterized HTML with applets/placeholders and no fixed answer choices; this needs a generated/adapted pipeline rather than direct wiring.
- Open Logic Project: strong fit for Formal Logic, but mostly proof/exercise prompts without playable answer choices; needs authored conversion.
- OpenTriviaQA Animals: one 20-question AP Biology micro-batch is now wired, but the full category should still not be bulk-imported. The remaining rows mix stable biology with conservation-status drift, Guinness/current records, vague "all of these" answers, and pet-trivia rows.

## Current Rule

Only wire sources that are already playable as fixed questions: prompt, correct answer, at least two distinct wrong answers, and enough context in the prompt. Everything else stays documented as source material for later conversion rather than being pushed into the live app.
