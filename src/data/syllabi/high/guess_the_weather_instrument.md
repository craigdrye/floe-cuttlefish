# Guess the Weather Instrument

## Course Shape
This meteorology course identifies weather instruments by what they measure and how the measurement helps describe the atmosphere.

## Chapters
- Temperature, pressure, and humidity
- Wind speed and wind direction
- Rainfall and precipitation measurement
- Solar radiation and cloud-base height

## Key Skills
- Match each instrument to the variable it measures
- Connect measurements to everyday weather forecasts
- Distinguish simple field tools from specialist meteorology equipment
