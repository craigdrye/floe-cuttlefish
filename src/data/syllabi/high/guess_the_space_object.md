# Guess the Space Object

## Course Shape
This astronomy course identifies planets, moons, small bodies, dwarf planets, and nebulae from memorable physical and orbital clues.

## Chapters
- Planets and dwarf planets: Mars, Saturn, and Pluto
- Ocean and atmosphere moons: Europa and Titan
- Small bodies: comets and asteroids
- Deep-sky objects: nebulae

## Key Skills
- Separate planets, moons, small bodies, and deep-sky objects
- Use composition, orbit, atmosphere, rings, tails, and surface clues
- Build sky curiosity without needing advanced astrophysics first
