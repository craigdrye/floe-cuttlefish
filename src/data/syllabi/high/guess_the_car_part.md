# Guess the Car Part

## Course Shape
This visual course teaches car-part literacy by system: cooling, charging, ignition, braking, exhaust, suspension, steering, and engine internals.

## Chapters
- Engine bay: radiator and alternator
- Ignition and combustion: spark plug and piston
- Braking systems: calipers and rotors
- Exhaust and emissions: catalytic converter
- Ride and steering: shocks and tie rods

## Key Skills
- Connect part shape to system function
- Distinguish similar mechanical components by what they do
- Build enough vocabulary to follow a mechanic’s explanation
