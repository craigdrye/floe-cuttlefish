# Guess the Aircraft Part

## Course Shape
This aviation-identification course teaches aircraft parts through lift, control surfaces, landing systems, fuselage structure, and engine clues.

## Chapters
- Lift and main structure: wings and fuselage
- Control surfaces: rudder, aileron, elevator, and flaps
- Ground operations: landing gear
- Propulsion: turbofan engines

## Key Skills
- Connect aircraft parts to flight forces and control axes
- Distinguish lift, pitch, yaw, and roll functions
- Read engineering clues without needing advanced aerodynamics first
