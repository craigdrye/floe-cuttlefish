# Guess the Sports Equipment

## Course Shape
This course identifies sports equipment from shape, material, use, and the game situation where it appears.

## Chapters
- Racket sports: tennis rackets and shuttlecocks
- Stick and puck sports: hockey and lacrosse
- Bat sports: cricket and baseball
- Target and protective gear: golf clubs and shin guards

## Key Skills
- Match equipment to sport and rule context
- Identify shape-function clues
- Learn sport vocabulary without needing to be an expert fan
