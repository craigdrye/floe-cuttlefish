# Guess the Lab Glassware

## Course Shape
This science-tools course identifies lab containers and liquid-handling tools by shape, precision, and use.

## Chapters
- Holding and mixing: beakers and Erlenmeyer flasks
- Measuring volume: graduated cylinders and volumetric flasks
- Small samples and transfer: test tubes and pipettes
- Titration and evaporation: burettes and watch glasses

## Key Skills
- Choose glassware by purpose and precision
- Read shape clues such as neck, spout, markings, and stopcock
- Build lab vocabulary before doing experiments
