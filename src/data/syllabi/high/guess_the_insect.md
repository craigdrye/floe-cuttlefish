# Guess the Insect

## Course Shape
This visual-identification course teaches insects through body plan, wings, legs, antennae, behavior, sound, and ecological role.

## Chapters
- Pollinators: honeybees and butterflies
- Aerial predators: dragonflies
- Beetles and garden helpers
- Noisy insects and jumpers: cicadas and grasshoppers
- Ambush predators and night fliers: mantises and moths

## Key Skills
- Use body structure and behavior as identification clues
- Distinguish insects by wings, legs, antennae, and role
- Connect insects to ecology without treating every unfamiliar insect as harmful
