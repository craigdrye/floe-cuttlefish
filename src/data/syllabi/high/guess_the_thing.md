# Guess the Thing

## Course Shape
This is the mixed visual slot-machine course: a broad sampler where the learner may land on a rock, fossil, coprolite, seaweed, strange fruit, country shape, city skyline, historical ruin, plumbing part, electrical part, car part, train part, architecture style, hand tool, kitchen tool, dog breed, cat breed, road sign, pasta shape, cloud type, flag, spice, cheese, art movement, camera part, music notation, sports equipment, lab glassware, insect, mushroom, constellation, gemstone, tree, weather instrument, aircraft part, medical instrument, circuit component, space object, surgical tool, dinosaur, knot, machine-shop tool, microscope part, toxic plant, bridge type, typography term, sewing tool, dental tool, plant, bird, animal, fish, body part, meme format, weird instrument, houseplant, literary character, or famous historical figure. Each question uses the paired cute-cartoon and literal-clue format with kids, adults, and experts difficulty tags.

## Key Skills
- Notice visual details before naming the object
- Transfer from playful cartoon clues to more literal field-guide clues
- Compare lookalikes across domains without overfitting to one obvious feature
- Build broad visual literacy: nature, geography, archaeology, paleontology, practical systems, electronics, infrastructure, design, shop craft, medicine, anatomy, aviation, meteorology, microscopy, music, astronomy, mineralogy, fungi, entomology, literature, history, digital culture, and everyday science
