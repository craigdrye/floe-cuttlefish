# Guess the Plant

## Course Shape
This is a region-aware visual plant ID course. The specimen slot lands on cartoon leaves, flowers, trunks, pods, or growth forms from different parts of the world.

## Chapters
- North America: sugar maple and saguaro cactus
- Latin America: monstera and cacao
- Europe: lavender and English oak
- Asia: bamboo and cherry blossom
- Oceania and Africa: eucalyptus and baobab

## Key Skills
- Read leaf shape, flower form, fruit or pod clues, and overall growth habit
- Link plants to native regions and habitats
- Separate lookalikes by using two or more field marks
