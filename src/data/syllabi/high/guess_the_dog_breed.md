# Guess the Dog Breed

## Course Shape
This course identifies dog breeds through body shape, coat, working history, and classic silhouette traits.

## Chapters
- Herding dogs: Border Collie and Corgi
- Scent and sight hounds: Beagle and Greyhound
- Northern working dogs: Siberian Husky
- Companion dogs: Pug and Poodle
- Body-shape specialists: Dachshund

## Key Skills
- Use physical traits and historical role as clues
- Avoid relying only on cuteness or popularity
- Connect breed shape to original work and behavior tendencies
