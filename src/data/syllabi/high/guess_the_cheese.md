# Guess the Cheese

## Course Shape
This course identifies cheeses through rind, texture, aging, melt behavior, shape, and classic culinary use.

## Chapters
- Blue and bloomy-rind cheeses
- Hard grating cheeses and fresh melting cheeses
- Crumbly and brined cheeses
- Aged wheels and fresh spoonable cheeses

## Key Skills
- Use texture, rind, and use-case clues
- Distinguish cheese families without needing expert tasting vocabulary
- Connect food structure to cooking behavior
