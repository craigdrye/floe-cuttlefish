# Guess the Kitchen Tool

## Course Shape
This course teaches kitchen-tool recognition from shape, food task, and safe use.

## Chapters
- Prep tools: microplanes and mandolines
- Mixing and serving: whisks and ladles
- Heat and draining: spider strainers and tongs
- Baking tools: bench scrapers and offset spatulas
- Safety: sharp tools and hot liquid handling

## Key Skills
- Identify tools by job and shape
- Explain why a tool is safer or better for a task
- Build cooking vocabulary that transfers to real kitchens
