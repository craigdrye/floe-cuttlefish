# Guess the Microscope Part

## Course Shape
This lab-literacy course identifies microscope parts by magnification, slide support, lighting, focusing, and structural clues.

## Chapters
- Viewing and magnification: eyepiece and objective lenses
- Slide support: stage and clips
- Light control: condenser and diaphragm
- Focusing and switching power: coarse focus, fine focus, and nosepiece

## Key Skills
- Match each microscope part to its role
- Use correct lab vocabulary while handling equipment
- Understand how light, focus, and magnification work together
