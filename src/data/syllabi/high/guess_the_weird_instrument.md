# Guess the Weird Instrument

## Course Shape
This is a playful music-culture course where students identify unusual instruments from construction, playing method, sound source, and cultural context.

## Chapters
- Electronic oddities: theremin and touchless control
- Cranks and strings: hurdy-gurdy and keyed fiddles
- Glass and metal sounds: glass armonica and Hang drum
- Wind drones and vessel flutes: didgeridoo, crumhorn, and ocarina
- Lutes and resonance: oud, sitar, sympathetic strings, and fretless designs

## Key Skills
- Classify instruments by how sound is produced
- Use playing posture, materials, and mechanism as clues
- Connect instruments to musical traditions without flattening them into novelty
