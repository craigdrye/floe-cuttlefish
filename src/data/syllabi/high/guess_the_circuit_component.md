# Guess the Circuit Component

## Course Shape
This electronics-literacy course identifies circuit components by what they do to current, voltage, charge, light, magnetic fields, and protection.

## Chapters
- Passive components: resistors, capacitors, and inductors
- One-way and light-emitting parts: diodes and LEDs
- Switching and amplification: transistors
- Protection and control: fuses and potentiometers

## Key Skills
- Match a component to its function in a circuit
- Use physical clues such as bands, leads, coils, and knobs
- Build electronics vocabulary before formal circuit analysis
