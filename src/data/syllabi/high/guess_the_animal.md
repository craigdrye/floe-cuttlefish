# Guess the Animal

## Course Shape
This is a general animal visual-ID course. The slot lands on cartoon silhouettes that emphasize body plan, movement, and signature adaptations.

## Chapters
- Mammals: fox, deer, elephant, giraffe, kangaroo, bear, rabbit, bat, and dolphin
- Reptiles: snake and turtle
- Amphibians: frog

## Key Skills
- Identify animals from silhouette and adaptation clues
- Connect structure to movement, feeding, habitat, or defense
- Compare near-miss animals without relying on vibes alone
