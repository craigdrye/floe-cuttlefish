# Guess the Pasta Shape

## Course Shape
This food-culture course identifies pasta shapes through geometry, texture, filling, and sauce-holding function.

## Chapters
- Long pasta: spaghetti and fettuccine
- Tube pasta: penne and rigatoni
- Small and shaped pasta: orzo, farfalle, and fusilli
- Stuffed pasta: ravioli

## Key Skills
- Identify food by shape and structure
- Connect pasta geometry to sauce and cooking use
- Build culinary vocabulary without being fussy about it
