# Guess the Electrical Part

## Course Shape
This course teaches basic electrical-part recognition from safe visual clues. It is about literacy and safety vocabulary, not DIY live electrical work.

## Chapters
- Switches and outlets
- Breakers and GFCI protection
- Wire connectors and grounding
- Boxes and conduit
- Safety clues: what a part is for before what it looks like

## Key Skills
- Recognize common electrical components
- Explain the safety role of breakers, grounding, and GFCIs
- Use precise vocabulary while respecting electrical hazards
