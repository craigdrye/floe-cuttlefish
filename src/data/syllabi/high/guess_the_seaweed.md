# Guess the Seaweed

## Course Shape
This visual-ID course teaches seaweed and algae through playful clue cards: shape, habitat, food use, floating structures, and ecological role.

## Chapters
- Brown seaweeds: kelp, sargassum, and bladderwrack
- Edible seaweeds: nori, kombu, wakame, and dulse
- Green seaweeds: sea lettuce and sheet-like forms
- Red seaweeds: Irish moss and food thickeners
- Reef builders: coralline algae and calcified crusts

## Key Skills
- Use habitat, shape, color, and use-case clues before guessing
- Distinguish floating, attached, edible, and calcified forms
- Connect seaweeds to ecology, food culture, and coastal systems
