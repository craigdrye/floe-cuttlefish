# Guess the Strange Fruit

## Course Shape
This edible-botany course teaches unusual fruits through visual clues, food uses, texture, smell, region, and plant-family hints. The goal is curiosity: unfamiliar produce is not automatically weird in a bad way.

## Chapters
- Spiky fruits: dragon fruit and rambutan
- Big tropical fruits: jackfruit and durian
- Tropical custards and purple shells: cherimoya and mangosteen
- Alien shapes: kiwano, Buddha’s hand, and starfruit
- Citrus and perfume: kumquat, yuzu, and feijoa

## Key Skills
- Use skin texture, interior structure, aroma, and food use as clues
- Separate sensory description from cultural judgment
- Connect fruits to plant families, regions, and culinary traditions
