# Guess the Rock

## Course Shape
This is a visual field-identification course built around a specimen-slot format. Each question shows a cartoon rock or mineral and asks students to identify it using visible evidence rather than memorized names.

## Chapters
- Igneous rocks: basalt, granite, obsidian, and pumice
- Sedimentary rocks: sandstone, limestone, and conglomerate
- Metamorphic rocks: slate, marble, and gneiss
- Minerals: quartz and amethyst

## Key Skills
- Distinguish grain size, banding, glassy texture, vesicles, and crystal habit
- Connect visual traits to formation stories
- Avoid one-clue overconfidence by checking texture, color, and context together
