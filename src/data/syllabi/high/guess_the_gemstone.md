# Guess the Gemstone

## Course Shape
This materials-science course identifies gemstones by color, mineral family, luster, inclusions, crystal habit, and origin.

## Chapters
- Corundum gems: sapphire and ruby
- Green and purple gems: emerald, peridot, and amethyst
- Organic and optical gems: pearl and opal
- Quartz gems: citrine

## Key Skills
- Connect gem names to mineral families
- Use color carefully without overtrusting it
- Notice inclusions, luster, habit, and origin clues
