# Guess the Constellation

## Course Shape
This sky-literacy course identifies constellations and famous asterisms by star pattern, season, bright stars, and neighboring sky landmarks.

## Chapters
- Northern sky anchors: Big Dipper and Cassiopeia
- Winter sky: Orion and Taurus
- Star clusters and small constellations: Pleiades and Lyra
- Summer and zodiac patterns: Cygnus and Scorpius

## Key Skills
- Read star patterns without needing perfect drawings
- Use bright stars and season as clues
- Distinguish constellation, asterism, and star cluster
