# Guess the Fish

## Course Shape
This course uses cartoon fish specimens and regional clues to build practical fish ID. It includes freshwater, marine, reef, farmed, and ornamental examples.

## Chapters
- North America: largemouth bass and rainbow trout
- Latin America: piranha and discus
- Europe and Asia: Atlantic cod, common carp, koi, and clownfish
- Africa and Oceania: Nile tilapia and barramundi

## Key Skills
- Read body shape, mouth size, bands, spots, barbels, and fin profile
- Connect fish to native regions and habitats
- Distinguish ecological ID from aquarium familiarity
