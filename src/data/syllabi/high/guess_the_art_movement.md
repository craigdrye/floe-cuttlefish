# Guess the Art Movement

## Course Shape
This art-history course identifies movements through visual style, subject matter, composition, materials, and historical attitude.

## Chapters
- Impressionism, Cubism, and Surrealism
- Pop Art and mass culture
- Baroque drama and Romantic emotion
- Minimalism and Rococo decoration

## Key Skills
- Identify style clues from composition and visual language
- Distinguish similar art movements by purpose and effect
- Explain what an artwork is trying to do, not just how it looks
