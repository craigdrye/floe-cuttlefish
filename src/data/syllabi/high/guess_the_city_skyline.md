# Guess the City Skyline

## Course Shape
This urban-geography course identifies cities through stylized skyline clues: towers, bridges, harbors, domes, mountains, rivers, and iconic buildings.

## Chapters
- Skyscraper skylines: New York City and Toronto
- Tower landmarks: Paris, Seattle, Dubai, and London
- Harbor cities: Sydney, Hong Kong, and San Francisco
- Ancient and sacred skylines: Rome and Istanbul
- Mountain and water cities: Rio de Janeiro

## Key Skills
- Match city identity to skyline, setting, and landmark clues
- Read built environment without needing photo-realistic images
- Connect landmarks to geography, history, and urban form
