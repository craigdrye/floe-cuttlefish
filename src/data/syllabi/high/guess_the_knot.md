# Guess the Knot

## Course Shape
This practical-skills course identifies knots by structure, purpose, loading behavior, and when a knot is or is not appropriate.

## Chapters
- Stopper knots: overhand and figure-eight
- Loops and binding knots: bowline and square knot
- Hitches and bends: clove hitch and sheet bend
- Friction and line knots: Prusik and fisherman’s knot

## Key Skills
- Match knot form to purpose
- Notice when a knot is convenient but not safe for critical loads
- Build outdoor and workshop vocabulary with safety in view
