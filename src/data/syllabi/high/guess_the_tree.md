# Guess the Tree

## Course Shape
This field-identification course teaches trees through leaves, needles, cones, seeds, bark, crown shape, habitat, and seasonal clues.

## Chapters
- Needles and cones: pine and cedar
- Seeds and leaves: oak, maple, and ginkgo
- Bark clues: birch and sycamore
- Water-loving forms: willow

## Key Skills
- Identify trees from more than one clue instead of one leaf shape
- Use bark, seeds, cones, and habitat as supporting evidence
- Build practical outdoor vocabulary for common trees
