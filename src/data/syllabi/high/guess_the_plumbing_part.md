# Guess the Plumbing Part

## Course Shape
This practical visual-ID course teaches common plumbing parts by shape, location, function, and what problem they solve.

## Chapters
- Pipe fittings: elbows and tees
- Under-sink systems: P-traps and cleanouts
- Valves and shutoffs: isolating fixtures
- Toilet parts: wax rings and flappers
- Faucet parts: aerators and small screens

## Key Skills
- Identify common parts before calling them “the pipe thing”
- Connect shape to water flow, sealing, venting, and access
- Learn repair vocabulary without touching anything dangerous
