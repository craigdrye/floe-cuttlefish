# Guess the Train Part

## Course Shape
This rail-systems course identifies train and track parts through visual clues, location, function, and movement.

## Chapters
- Track basics: rails, sleepers, and ballast
- Rolling stock: bogies and couplers
- Electric trains: pantographs and power collection
- Track routing: switch points
- Operations: signals and movement authority

## Key Skills
- Identify core rail infrastructure and vehicle parts
- Explain how trains are guided, powered, connected, and routed
- Read transport systems as engineered networks
