# Guess the Country Shape

## Course Shape
This geography course teaches country recognition through stylized outline clues, relative location, coastlines, island chains, peninsulas, and famous map-shape nicknames.

## Chapters
- Iconic outlines: Italy and France
- Island countries: Japan, New Zealand, and Sri Lanka
- Huge countries: Australia, Canada, and Brazil
- Long and peninsula countries: Chile and India
- Archipelagos and regional anchors: Indonesia and South Africa

## Key Skills
- Recognize countries from silhouette and geographic context
- Use coastlines, islands, peninsulas, and neighboring regions as clues
- Avoid relying only on memorized flag or capital facts
