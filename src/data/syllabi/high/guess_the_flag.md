# Guess the Flag

## Course Shape
This geography course identifies flags through color, layout, shape, symbol, and regional design families.

## Chapters
- Nordic crosses: Denmark and Sweden
- Simple central symbols: Canada and Japan
- Tricolors: France and Italy
- Stars, stripes, crescents, and other symbols

## Key Skills
- Read flag design from layout and symbol clues
- Compare similar flags carefully
- Connect symbols to geography without reducing countries to trivia
