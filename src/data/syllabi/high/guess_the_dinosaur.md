# Guess the Dinosaur

## Course Shape
This paleontology course identifies dinosaurs by fossil-form clues: plates, horns, teeth, neck posture, crests, armor, sails, claws, and body plan.

## Chapters
- Classic herbivores: Stegosaurus, Triceratops, and Brachiosaurus
- Armor and display: Ankylosaurus and Parasaurolophus
- Large predators: Tyrannosaurus rex and Spinosaurus
- Small theropods: Velociraptor

## Key Skills
- Use body plan rather than movie memory alone
- Connect visible structures to possible behavior or classification
- Distinguish broad dinosaur groups from one iconic feature
