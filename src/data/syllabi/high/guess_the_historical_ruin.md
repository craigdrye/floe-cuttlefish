# Guess the Historical Ruin

## Course Shape
This course identifies major ruins and archaeological sites through paired cartoon and literal clue cards. Each question asks learners to connect shape, architecture, location, era, and original function.

## Chapters
- Ancient Mediterranean: Colosseum, Parthenon, and Pompeii
- Rock-cut and mountain sites: Petra and Machu Picchu
- Maya and Mesoamerica: Chichen Itza and Tikal
- South and Southeast Asia: Angkor Wat
- Ancient Egypt and the Near East: Karnak, Abu Simbel, and Persepolis
- North American cliff dwellings: Mesa Verde

## Key Skills
- Identify ruins from architectural form, setting, and cultural context
- Distinguish similar ancient sites using specific clues
- Explain what a ruin was for, not just what it looks like
- Treat heritage sites with curiosity and respect
