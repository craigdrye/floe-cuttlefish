# Guess the Camera Part

## Course Shape
This creative-tech course identifies camera parts through their shape, location, and role in making an image.

## Chapters
- Optics: lenses and lens openings
- Exposure: shutter and aperture
- Viewing and recording: viewfinders and sensors
- Accessories and storage: hot shoes, card slots, and tripod sockets

## Key Skills
- Connect camera parts to light, timing, framing, and storage
- Use precise photography vocabulary
- Understand what a camera is doing before pressing buttons
