# Guess the Spice

## Course Shape
This food-culture course identifies spices through form, aroma, source plant part, color, and common culinary use.

## Chapters
- Pods and seeds: cardamom and cumin
- Threads and powders: saffron, turmeric, and paprika
- Bark and buds: cinnamon and cloves
- Star-shaped spices: star anise

## Key Skills
- Use plant part, shape, and color as clues
- Connect spices to food traditions without stereotyping them
- Build sensory vocabulary for cooking
