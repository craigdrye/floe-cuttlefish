# Guess the Fossil

## Course Shape
This is a playful visual fossil-identification course using the paired cute-cartoon and literal-clue slot format. It includes classic body fossils, plant fossils, trace fossils, and a deliberately generous coprolite unit.

## Chapters
- Classic fossils: trilobites, ammonites, and brachiopods
- Trace fossils: footprints and activity clues
- Plant fossils: leaf imprints and petrified wood
- Teeth and bones: shark teeth and dinosaur bones
- Coprolites: fossilized feces, diet clues, pellets, fragments, and lookalikes

## Key Skills
- Distinguish body fossils from trace fossils
- Use shape, texture, inclusions, and context before guessing
- Explain why coprolites are funny but scientifically useful
- Connect fossils to ancient behavior, diet, environment, and ecosystem evidence
