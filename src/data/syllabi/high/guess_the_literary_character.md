# Guess the Literary Character

## Course Shape
This literature course asks learners to identify characters from motive, conflict, symbol, setting, and narrative role rather than memorized plot trivia alone.

## Chapters
- Shakespeare and tragedy: Hamlet and Antigone
- Austen and the novel of manners: Elizabeth Bennet
- Detectives and Gothic narrators: Sherlock Holmes and Jane Eyre
- American novels: Gatsby, Scrooge, and Holden Caulfield
- Epic and satire: Odysseus and Don Quixote

## Key Skills
- Infer character identity from conflict and theme
- Distinguish similar archetypes through specific textual clues
- Connect a character to genre, era, and central moral problem
