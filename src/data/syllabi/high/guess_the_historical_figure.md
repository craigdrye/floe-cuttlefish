# Guess the Historical Figure

## Course Shape
This history course identifies major historical figures through context, contribution, era, and careful biographical clues. The tone is playful, but the aim is respectful and historically grounded.

## Chapters
- Ancient and medieval worlds: Cleopatra VII and Ibn Sina
- Science and computing: Ada Lovelace and Marie Curie
- Abolition and revolution: Frederick Douglass and Toussaint Louverture
- Civil war, resistance, and national movements: Lincoln, Joan of Arc, and Gandhi
- Civil rights: Rosa Parks and public action

## Key Skills
- Use era, place, contribution, and conflict as identification clues
- Avoid reducing people to single labels or trivia facts
- Explain why a figure matters in historical context
