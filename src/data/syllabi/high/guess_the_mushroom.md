# Guess the Mushroom

## Course Shape
This course teaches mushroom visual vocabulary using cap shape, gills, pores, ridges, shelves, teeth, color, and habitat. It is for learning, not for deciding what to eat.

## Chapters
- Iconic fungi: fly agaric
- Honeycomb and golden mushrooms: morels and chanterelles
- Shelf and cultivated mushrooms: oyster, shiitake, and button mushrooms
- Toothy and bolete forms: lion's mane and porcini
- Safety: why visual identification alone is not enough for foraging

## Key Skills
- Describe fungi using cap, stem, gill, pore, shelf, and tooth clues
- Separate curiosity from eating decisions
- Notice lookalike risk and when expert confirmation matters
