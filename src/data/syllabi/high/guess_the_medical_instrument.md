# Guess the Medical Instrument

## Course Shape
This clinical-literacy course identifies common medical instruments by what they examine, measure, deliver, or support.

## Chapters
- Listening and vital signs: stethoscope, blood pressure cuff, and pulse oximeter
- Ear and eye exams: otoscope and ophthalmoscope
- Neurological and airway tools: reflex hammer and laryngoscope
- Fluid handling: syringe

## Key Skills
- Match tools to clinical tasks
- Use exam context as an identification clue
- Build healthcare vocabulary without pretending to diagnose
