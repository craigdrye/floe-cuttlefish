# Guess the Music Notation

## Course Shape
This music-literacy course identifies common notation symbols by appearance and function.

## Chapters
- Clefs: treble and bass
- Accidentals: sharp, flat, and related pitch changes
- Rhythm: quarter notes, eighth notes, and rests
- Structure: time signatures and measure grouping

## Key Skills
- Read common score symbols visually
- Connect notation to pitch, rhythm, silence, and meter
- Build vocabulary for learning instruments and reading music
