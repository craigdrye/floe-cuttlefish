# Guess the Dental Tool

## Course Shape
This healthcare-vocabulary course identifies dental tools by inspection, probing, cleaning, suction, rinsing, grasping, gumline care, and patient support.

## Chapters
- Seeing and probing: dental mirrors and explorers
- Cleaning and gumline tools: scalers and curettes
- Fluid control: air-water syringes and saliva ejectors
- Support and handling: cotton pliers and bite blocks

## Key Skills
- Connect dental instrument shape to clinical task
- Use precise vocabulary for common dental-care tools
- Understand tool roles without pretending to diagnose or perform care
