# Guess the Road Sign

## Course Shape
This practical course teaches road signs by color, shape, symbol, and the action they require.

## Chapters
- Regulatory signs: stop, yield, do not enter, one way, and no U-turn
- Warning signs: pedestrian crossing, railroad crossing, and merge
- Shape literacy: octagons, triangles, arrows, and symbol panels
- Safety decisions: what to do next

## Key Skills
- Read signs from shape and symbol, not just words
- Connect signs to safe behavior
- Build everyday navigation and road-safety literacy
