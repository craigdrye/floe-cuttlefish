# Guess the Cat Breed

## Course Shape
This visual-ID course teaches cat breeds by coat, face shape, ears, pattern, body type, and common breed-history clues.

## Chapters
- Longhair and giant cats: Maine Coon and Persian
- Colorpoint cats: Siamese and Ragdoll
- Unusual coats: Sphynx, Bengal, Russian Blue, and Devon Rex
- Trait clues: ears, eyes, coat texture, and body shape

## Key Skills
- Identify breed clues from visible traits
- Separate breed stereotypes from actual individual animals
- Use precise animal-description vocabulary
