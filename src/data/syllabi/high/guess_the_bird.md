# Guess the Bird

## Course Shape
This course turns bird identification into a quick visual slot game. Students use color, bill shape, posture, body shape, and habitat clues to name the bird.

## Chapters
- Garden birds: robin, cardinal, and blue jay
- Water birds: mallard and Canada goose
- Tiny birds and tree birds: hummingbird and woodpecker
- Raptors and night birds: bald eagle and barn owl
- Sea and wading birds: penguin, flamingo, and great blue heron

## Key Skills
- Use silhouette before color when possible
- Match bill shape to feeding style
- Combine plumage, habitat, and body plan instead of guessing from one bright color
