# Guess the Houseplant

## Course Shape
This course teaches indoor plant identification with visual clues, care clues, and the tiny domestic drama of figuring out why a plant is unhappy.

## Chapters
- Leaf shapes: monstera, fiddle-leaf fig, and rubber plant
- Vines and plantlets: pothos and spider plant
- Tough survivors: snake plant and ZZ plant
- Flowers and spathes: peace lily
- Pattern leaves and succulents: calathea and aloe

## Key Skills
- Identify plants from leaf shape, growth habit, and texture
- Link care needs to visible traits without overgeneralizing
- Notice common lookalikes and choose the most specific clue
