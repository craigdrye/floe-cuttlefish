# Dinosaur World (Pre-school, ages 3–5)
**ID**: `dinosPreschool` · **Status**: playable

## Module 1: Meet the Dinosaurs (25%)
- What is a dinosaur? Ancient reptiles, lived millions of years ago
- Famous dinos: T-Rex, Triceratops, Stegosaurus, Brachiosaurus, Pteranodon
- Size comparison: "As big as a bus" / "as small as a chicken"
- Dino sounds and movements (stomp, roar, fly, swim)
- **Questions**: Name the dino from a picture, match dino to silhouette

## Module 2: What Did They Eat? (25%)
- Herbivore = plant eater (flat teeth, long necks) — Brachiosaurus, Triceratops
- Carnivore = meat eater (sharp teeth, claws) — T-Rex, Velociraptor
- Omnivore = eats both
- Matching teeth shape to diet
- **Questions**: "Is this dino a plant eater or meat eater?", sort by diet

## Module 3: Fossils & Discovery (25%)
- What is a fossil? Bones/footprints turned to rock
- Paleontologists = dinosaur detectives
- Where fossils are found (in rocks, underground)
- Fossil dig concept — brushing away sand
- **Questions**: "What is a fossil?", "Who studies dinosaurs?"

## Module 4: When Dinosaurs Disappeared (25%)
- Dinosaurs are not alive today (extinct)
- Simple explanation: big asteroid, world changed
- Some relatives still here: birds are living dinosaurs!
- **Questions**: "Are dinosaurs alive today?", "What animal is related to dinosaurs?"

**Stretch Zone**: Introduce Triassic/Jurassic/Cretaceous as "chapters"; flying reptiles vs dinosaurs; marine reptiles
