# Pre-K through grade 2 basics (Collection)
**ID**: `col-pre-k-through-grade-2-basics` · **Discipline**: Kids Discovery

## Course Philosophy
This is the broadest generalist collection for the youngest users, covering
the full pre-K to 2nd grade spectrum. It functions as a "sample platter"
that introduces children to the joy of learning across multiple domains. Questions
should be play-oriented, visual, and forgiving.

## Syllabus Overview
Since this is a collection-type course (dynamically generated from the Kids
Discovery group), the syllabus maps to the following domains:

**Domain A: Early Numeracy (30%)**
- Counting 1–20
- Number recognition
- Simple addition and subtraction within 5
- Shape recognition (2D)
- Patterns (AB, ABB, ABC)
- Measurement vocabulary (big/small, tall/short, heavy/light)

**Domain B: Early Literacy (30%)**
- Letter recognition (upper and lower case)
- Letter-sound correspondence (phonics)
- Rhyming
- High-frequency sight words (10–20 words)
- Story comprehension (who, what, where)
- Vocabulary (colours, animals, body parts, food, clothing)

**Domain C: World Knowledge (20%)**
- Community helpers (doctor, teacher, firefighter)
- Seasons and weather
- Maps and where things are (my house, my school, my country)
- Celebrations and holidays from around the world
- Animals and their homes

**Domain D: Social & Emotional Learning (20%)**
- Naming feelings
- Taking turns and sharing
- Being a good friend
- Understanding rules
- Kindness and helpfulness
- Dealing with frustration

**Stretch Zone**:
- Simple coding concepts (sequencing instructions for a robot)
- Introduction to time (morning, afternoon, night; days of the week)
- Money awareness (coins exist, things cost money)
