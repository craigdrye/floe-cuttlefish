# Up to 2nd grade skills (Collection)
**ID**: `col-up-to-2nd-grade-skills` · **Discipline**: Kids Discovery

## Syllabus Overview
This collection targets the upper end of the pre-school bracket — children
who are at or approaching the 2nd grade level. Content should feel like a
graduation from the "basics" collection above.

**Domain A: Maths Fluency (30%)**
- Addition and subtraction within 10
- Skip counting by 2s, 5s, 10s
- Place value (tens and ones)
- Telling time to the hour
- Simple measurement (length comparison, non-standard units)
- Introduction to fractions (half, quarter — visual/concrete)

**Domain B: Reading & Writing (30%)**
- Decoding CVC and CVCC words
- Reading simple sentences
- Comprehension: "What happened in the story?"
- Writing: Complete a sentence with the correct word
- Punctuation awareness (full stop, question mark)
- Vocabulary expansion (synonyms, antonyms at a basic level)

**Domain C: Science & Discovery (20%)**
- Properties of materials (hard/soft, shiny/dull, rough/smooth)
- Forces: push and pull
- Light and shadow
- Sound: loud/quiet, high/low
- Living vs. non-living things
- Magnets (attract/repel at a conceptual level)

**Domain D: Social Studies & SEL (20%)**
- My neighbourhood: who lives and works here
- Rules at home vs. rules at school
- Needs vs. wants
- Recycling and taking care of the environment
- Respecting differences
- Problem-solving in social situations

**Stretch Zone**:
- Introduction to multiplication as "groups of"
- Reading short paragraphs and answering inference questions
- Basic map reading (compass directions, simple keys)
- Scientific method as a 3-step process: Question → Test → What happened?
