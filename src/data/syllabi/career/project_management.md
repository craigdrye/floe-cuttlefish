## Course Philosophy
This syllabus focuses on execution discipline: defining scope, sequencing work, tracking risks, and driving alignment without losing control of timeline or quality.

## Module 1: Planning and Scoping
- Goals, deliverables, assumptions, dependencies, and constraints
- Work breakdown structures and milestone design
- Scope boundaries and change-request discipline

## Module 2: Schedule and Risk Control
- Critical path, bottlenecks, buffers, and resource contention
- Risk registers, mitigation plans, and escalation thresholds
- Status reporting that distinguishes signal from noise

## Module 3: Team Operating Rhythm
- Meeting hygiene, action ownership, and follow-through
- Cross-functional coordination and expectation management
- Retrospectives, lessons learned, and recovery after slippage

## Stretch Zone
- Portfolio prioritization, vendor management, and program-level governance
