# Regulatory Affairs
**ID**: `regulatory` · **Discipline**: Healthcare

## Course Philosophy
This syllabus trains regulatory professionals to think in terms of evidence, claims discipline, submission quality, and inspection-ready execution.

## Module 1: Regulatory Pathways and Evidence
- Product classification, intended use, and pathway selection
- Labeling, claims, indications, and evidence thresholds
- How risk class changes documentation and review burden

## Module 2: Submission Strategy and Change Control
- Submission components, traceability, and gap analysis
- Design changes, document control, and version discipline
- Working across quality, clinical, engineering, and marketing teams

## Module 3: Compliance and Inspection Readiness
- CAPA logic, audit responses, and recurring documentation failures
- Post-market obligations, complaint handling, and vigilance basics
- Writing clearly enough that another reviewer can reconstruct decisions

## Stretch Zone
- Global pathway comparison, combination products, and borderline-claim cases
