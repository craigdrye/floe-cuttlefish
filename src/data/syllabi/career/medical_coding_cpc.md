# Medical Coding CPC
**ID**: `medicalCodingCpc` · **Discipline**: Healthcare

## Course Philosophy
This syllabus centers on documentation-based coding accuracy: extracting clinical meaning, assigning codes with specificity, and avoiding preventable claim errors.

## Module 1: Coding Foundations
- Diagnosis vs procedure coding logic
- Specificity, laterality, and encounter context
- Reading provider documentation for billable detail

## Module 2: Claims and Compliance
- Medical necessity, bundling concepts, and modifier judgment
- Common denial drivers and clean-claim discipline
- Payer-facing accuracy versus overcoding risk

## Module 3: Professional Practice
- Querying for clarification without inventing facts
- Audit readiness and defensible rationale
- Speed, quality, and consistency under production pressure

## Stretch Zone
- Specialty coding, reimbursement patterns, and audit strategy
