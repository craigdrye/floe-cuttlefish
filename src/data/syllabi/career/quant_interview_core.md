# Quant Interview Core
**ID**: `quant` · **Discipline**: Finance

## Course Philosophy
This syllabus prepares candidates for the first layer of quant screening: probability fluency, expected-value reasoning, combinatorics, estimation, and interview-style problem solving under pressure.

## Module 1: Probability and Counting
- Conditional probability, Bayes, independence, and expectation
- Counting methods, permutations, combinations, and symmetry
- Translating word problems into clean random-variable models

## Module 2: Brainteasers and Market Reasoning
- Classic interview puzzles, invariants, and elimination logic
- Fair games, edge cases, and expected-value framing
- Basic market intuition: payoffs, hedging ideas, and risk/reward tradeoffs

## Module 3: Core Technical Screen
- Linear algebra basics, calculus intuition, and simple optimization
- Algorithms, data structures, and implementation judgment
- Clear communication of assumptions, shortcuts, and checks

## Stretch Zone
- Stochastic-process intuition, simulation thinking, and timed mock interview reps
