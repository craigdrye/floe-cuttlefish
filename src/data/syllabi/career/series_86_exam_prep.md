# Series 86 Exam Prep
**ID**: `series86` · **Discipline**: Finance

## Course Philosophy
This syllabus prepares equity-research candidates to reason through valuation, accounting, industry dynamics, and defensible investment conclusions.

## Module 1: Accounting and Financial Statements
- Income statement, balance sheet, and cash flow linkage
- Revenue recognition, expense timing, and quality-of-earnings questions
- Ratio analysis, margins, and forecast sanity checks

## Module 2: Valuation and Research Logic
- Comparable-company analysis, precedent logic, and simple DCF framing
- EPS, dilution, capital structure, and valuation multiples
- Industry drivers, thesis formation, and catalyst identification

## Module 3: Research Judgment and Exam Discipline
- Reading disclosures, identifying key assumptions, and red flags
- Translating model outputs into investment conclusions
- Time management, question triage, and avoiding overcomplication

## Stretch Zone
- Sector-specific modeling nuance, earnings-bridge analysis, and variant-perception thinking
