# People Management
**ID**: `peopleManagement` · **Discipline**: Leadership

## Course Philosophy
This syllabus focuses on practical management: setting expectations, coaching performance, delegating well, and building a team operating system that actually works.

## Module 1: Manager Fundamentals
- Role clarity, ownership, and trust-building
- Setting goals, standards, and decision rights
- 1:1s that surface real issues instead of status theater

## Module 2: Feedback and Performance
- Clear feedback, difficult conversations, and follow-up
- Coaching versus directing
- Diagnosing skill gaps, motivation gaps, and system problems

## Module 3: Team Health and Scale
- Delegation, escalation, and avoiding bottlenecks
- Hiring signal, onboarding, and role calibration
- Conflict management and accountability without drama

## Stretch Zone
- Organizational design, succession planning, and managing managers
