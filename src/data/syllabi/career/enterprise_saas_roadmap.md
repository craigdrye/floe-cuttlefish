# Enterprise SaaS / B2B Tech Roadmap
**ID**: `enterpriseSaaSRoadmap` - **Discipline**: Technology - **Level**: Career

## Course Aim
Prepare learners to understand enterprise SaaS work language across product, sales, implementation, customer success, and revenue teams. The course translates discovery, stakeholders, enablement, onboarding, renewal risk, and churn language into concrete operating tasks.

## Course Design Notes
Route questions here when they test B2B SaaS workflows, discovery, implementation, enablement, customer health, expansion, churn, stakeholder mapping, or go-to-market handoffs. Emphasize the relationship between product value and commercial execution.

## Chapter 1: Buyers, Users, and Stakeholders
- **Key concepts**: economic buyer, champion, evaluator, admin, end user, procurement, legal.
- **Practice questions**: Who owns budget? Who feels pain? Who can block the deal?
- **Work output**: stakeholder map.

## Chapter 2: Discovery and Value Framing
- **Key concepts**: pain, use case, success criteria, workflow, ROI, urgency, business case.
- **Practice questions**: What problem is real? What outcome would justify buying?
- **Work output**: discovery summary with value hypothesis.

## Chapter 3: Implementation and Enablement
- **Key concepts**: onboarding, migration, integration, training, change management, enablement materials.
- **Practice questions**: What must happen before value appears? Who needs training?
- **Work output**: implementation plan with dependencies and risks.

## Chapter 4: Customer Health and Renewal Risk
- **Key concepts**: adoption, product usage, support burden, QBR, NPS, churn, renewal, expansion.
- **Practice questions**: Is the customer receiving value? What predicts downgrade or churn?
- **Work output**: customer-health review.

## Chapter 5: Internal Handoffs
- **Key concepts**: sales-to-CS handoff, product feedback, support escalation, roadmap request, deal desk.
- **Practice questions**: What context is missing? Who owns the next action?
- **Work output**: handoff checklist.

## Capstone
Build a customer journey pack from prospect to renewal: stakeholder map, value case, implementation plan, health risks, and expansion path.
