# Negotiation Skills
**ID**: `negotiation` · **Discipline**: Communication

## Course Philosophy
This syllabus teaches negotiation as structured problem-solving: clarifying interests, building leverage, and creating better options before claiming value.

## Module 1: Foundations
- Positions vs interests, BATNA, reservation point, and ZOPA
- Preparation, information gathering, and counterpart analysis
- When to anchor, when to withhold, and when to reframe

## Module 2: Conversation Control
- Asking diagnostic questions and testing assumptions
- Managing tone, tempo, and concessions
- Using packages, tradeoffs, and conditional offers

## Module 3: Closing and Relationship Quality
- Creating durable agreements and reducing ambiguity
- Handling deadlock, escalation, and bad-faith signals
- Preserving trust while still protecting your outcome

## Stretch Zone
- Compensation negotiation, enterprise procurement, and multi-party settings
