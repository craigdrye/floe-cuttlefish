# Sales Fundamentals
**ID**: `salesFundamentals` · **Discipline**: Commercial

## Course Philosophy
This syllabus emphasizes disciplined selling: discovery first, value framing second, and closing only after fit is clear.

## Module 1: Discovery and Qualification
- Asking good questions and diagnosing real pain
- Stakeholders, buying process, urgency, and budget
- Separating curiosity from real opportunity

## Module 2: Value and Objections
- Positioning, ROI framing, and outcome language
- Handling objections without becoming defensive
- Social proof, risk reduction, and trust

## Module 3: Deal Progression
- Mutual next steps, closing signals, and forecast hygiene
- Pipeline quality and deal review logic
- Learning from losses without rewriting history

## Stretch Zone
- Multi-threading, procurement, and expansion strategy
