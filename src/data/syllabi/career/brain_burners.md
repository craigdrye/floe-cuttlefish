# Brain Burners
**ID**: `brainBurners` · **Discipline**: Logic / Puzzles · **Stage**: Career Hopper / Adult Enrichment

## Purpose
Brain Burners is Floe's puzzle-first reasoning lane. It strips out the finance wrapper and keeps the part many learners actually want: clean, satisfying thinking under constraint.

## What Learners Practice
- Spotting invariants, parity constraints, and hidden counting structure
- Turning wordy setups into smaller solvable subproblems
- Using elimination, contradiction, and case-splitting without getting lost
- Recognizing recurring puzzle families such as scale puzzles, river crossings, encoded measurements, and information traps

## Internal Chapter Arc
1. Warm-up riddles with one clean unlock
2. Elimination and contradiction puzzles
3. Counting and combinatorics traps
4. Invariants, parity, and conservation tricks
5. Multi-step puzzle protocols and interview-style explanation

## Design Notes
- Keep the tone playful but not childish
- Favor puzzles that teach reusable reasoning moves over one-off trivia
- Explanations should name the general pattern so the learner can transfer it
- This course should sit beside maths and philosophy without feeling dependent on either
