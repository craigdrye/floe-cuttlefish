# Technical Sales Engineer Interview Prep
**ID**: `technicalSales` · **Discipline**: Commercial

## Course Philosophy
This syllabus prepares sales engineers to blend technical clarity with commercial judgment: discovery, demos, objection handling, and credible solution design.

## Module 1: Discovery and Qualification
- User pain, buying process, stakeholders, and success criteria
- Clarifying technical requirements without slipping into jargon
- Distinguishing curiosity from real buying intent

## Module 2: Demo, Solutioning, and Objections
- Tailoring product stories to business value and technical fit
- Handling security, integration, performance, and ROI objections
- Pilots, proof-of-concepts, and risk-reduction framing

## Module 3: Interview and Cross-Functional Execution
- Whiteboarding architectures and explaining tradeoffs simply
- Partnering with account executives, product, and engineering
- Post-call recap quality, follow-through, and trust-building

## Stretch Zone
- Procurement pressure, mutual action plans, and competitive displacement strategy
