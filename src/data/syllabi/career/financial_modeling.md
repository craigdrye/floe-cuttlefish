# Financial Modeling
**ID**: `financialModeling` · **Discipline**: Finance

## Course Philosophy
This syllabus builds practical modeling discipline: clear assumptions, statement logic, sensitivity analysis, and cash-flow reasoning that supports real decisions.

## Module 1: Model Foundations
- Revenue drivers, operating assumptions, and clean model structure
- Income statement, balance sheet, and cash flow statement linkage
- Error checks, sign conventions, and circular-reference awareness

## Module 2: Forecasting and Valuation Logic
- Margin assumptions, working capital, capex, and debt schedules
- Free cash flow and valuation intuition
- Sensitivity tables and scenario analysis

## Module 3: Communication and Judgment
- Making models readable for reviewers
- Stress-testing assumptions and identifying fragile outputs
- Explaining what the model says, and what it does not

## Stretch Zone
- LBO logic, merger models, and multi-segment forecasting
