# Public Speaking
**ID**: `publicSpeaking` · **Discipline**: Communication

## Course Philosophy
This syllabus helps learners speak clearly under pressure by improving structure, pacing, audience adaptation, and delivery confidence.

## Module 1: Message Design
- Clarifying the one thing the audience should remember
- Openings, transitions, and endings
- Simplifying complex ideas without flattening them

## Module 2: Delivery Mechanics
- Pace, pause, volume, and verbal emphasis
- Body language, eye contact, and slide support
- Handling nerves without losing clarity

## Module 3: Audience Adaptation
- Tailoring content to audience knowledge and stakes
- Reading the room and recovering from confusion
- Q&A handling and speaking with authority

## Stretch Zone
- Executive briefings, panels, and persuasive presentations
