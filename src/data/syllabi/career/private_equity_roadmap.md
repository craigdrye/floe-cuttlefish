# Private Equity Roadmap
**ID**: `privateEquityRoadmap` - **Discipline**: Finance - **Level**: Career

## Course Aim
Teach learners how private equity investors evaluate companies, underwrite returns, structure leverage, run diligence, and plan value creation. The goal is disciplined ownership thinking, not just LBO mechanics.

## Course Design Notes
Route questions here when they test PE diligence, LBO models, investment committee memos, debt capacity, management questions, value creation, exits, or portfolio monitoring. Keep the learner focused on cash flow, risk, and return.

## Chapter 1: PE Model and Investment Criteria
- **Key concepts**: sponsor, platform, add-on, control, hold period, IRR, MOIC, entry multiple, exit multiple.
- **Practice questions**: Why would this be a PE asset? What return does the deal need? What could break the thesis?
- **Work output**: investment-screen note with fit, risks, and initial return case.

## Chapter 2: Business Diligence
- **Key concepts**: market growth, customer concentration, recurring revenue, margin quality, pricing power, management depth.
- **Practice questions**: Is revenue durable? Where is customer risk? What diligence would confirm the thesis?
- **Work output**: diligence request list tied to thesis, risks, and decision points.

## Chapter 3: LBO Mechanics and Debt Capacity
- **Key concepts**: sources and uses, leverage, EBITDA, cash sweep, interest expense, covenants, sponsor equity.
- **Practice questions**: How much debt can the business support? What drives IRR? What happens in a downside case?
- **Work output**: LBO case summary with base, downside, and sensitivity drivers.

## Chapter 4: Value Creation and Operating Plan
- **Key concepts**: margin expansion, pricing, salesforce productivity, procurement, working capital, add-on acquisition.
- **Practice questions**: Which levers are controllable? What needs management buy-in? What timing is realistic?
- **Work output**: value-creation plan with initiatives, owners, timing, and EBITDA impact.

## Chapter 5: IC Memo, Deal Process, and Exit
- **Key concepts**: LOI, exclusivity, quality of earnings, legal diligence, IC memo, exit route, strategic buyer, sponsor-to-sponsor.
- **Practice questions**: What must IC believe? Which diligence item is gating? Who is the likely exit buyer?
- **Work output**: IC memo excerpt with thesis, valuation, diligence findings, risks, and exit plan.

## Capstone
Create a private equity deal pack for a fictional target: screening note, diligence list, LBO summary, value-creation plan, IC memo excerpt, and exit view.
