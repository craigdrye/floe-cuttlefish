# Clinical Research Coordinator
**ID**: `clinicalResearch` · **Discipline**: Healthcare

## Course Philosophy
This syllabus prepares coordinators to run studies with protocol discipline, participant protection, accurate source documentation, and operational calm.

## Module 1: Participant Safety and Consent
- Informed consent, eligibility, and participant rights
- Screening logic, inclusion/exclusion criteria, and documentation
- Handling protocol deviations without losing safety focus

## Module 2: Study Operations and Data Quality
- Visit scheduling, source documents, CRFs, and query resolution
- Adverse-event capture, concomitant medication tracking, and follow-up
- Coordination with investigators, sponsors, and monitors

## Module 3: Compliance and Closeout
- GCP habits, delegation logs, accountability, and filing discipline
- Audit readiness, monitoring preparation, and corrective actions
- Keeping timelines moving without compromising data integrity

## Stretch Zone
- Randomization, blinding, multi-site coordination, and startup bottlenecks
