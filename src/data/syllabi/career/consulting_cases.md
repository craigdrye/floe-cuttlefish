# Consulting Cases
**ID**: `consultingCases` · **Discipline**: Career Hopper

## Course Philosophy
This syllabus prepares learners for case interviews by emphasizing structure, quantitative calm, synthesis, and recommendation quality under time pressure.

## Module 1: Case Structure
- Clarifying the objective and defining the decision
- Issue trees, profit trees, market-entry frames, and operational frames
- Hypothesis-led reasoning rather than random brainstorming

## Module 2: Quantitative Analysis
- Revenue, cost, margin, market sizing, and breakeven math
- Estimation, sanity checking, and assumption management
- Reading charts and turning numbers into conclusions

## Module 3: Synthesis and Recommendation
- Distilling findings into an executive answer
- Linking evidence to recommendation and risk
- Handling pushback, new data, and interviewer nudges

## Stretch Zone
- Public sector, pricing, M&A, and operations-heavy case variants
