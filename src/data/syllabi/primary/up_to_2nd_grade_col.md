# Up to 2nd Grade (Collection)
**ID**: `col-up-to-2nd-grade-skills` · **Discipline**: Kids Discovery · **Year**: 1 (ages 5–6)

> This is a collection course that also appears in the Pre-school bucket.
> When shown to Year 1 students, questions should skew toward the upper
> end of the difficulty range described in the preschool syllabus.
> See `preschool/up_to_2nd_grade_skills.md` for the full syllabus.
> 
> **Year 1 focus areas**:
> - Addition and subtraction within 10 (moving toward 20)
> - Reading simple sentences and answering comprehension questions
> - Properties of materials (hard/soft, shiny/dull)
> - Rules at home vs school, needs vs wants
