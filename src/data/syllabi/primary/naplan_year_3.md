# NAPLAN Prep (Year 3)
**ID**: `naplanYear3` · **Discipline**: Exam Prep · **Year**: 2 (shown in Year 2 as prep for the Year 3 NAPLAN test)
**Framework**: Australian NAPLAN (National Assessment Program — Literacy and Numeracy)

## Course Philosophy
NAPLAN Year 3 is administered in March of Year 3, so preparation begins in Year 2. This course covers the four NAPLAN domains: **Reading, Writing, Language Conventions, and Numeracy**. Questions should mirror the CBT (computer-based test) format used since 2018.

## Module 1: Reading (25%)
- Read and comprehend short fiction and non-fiction passages
- Literal comprehension: who, what, where, when
- Inferential comprehension: "Why did the character do that?"
- Vocabulary in context: "What does the word ___ mean in this sentence?"
- Text features: headings, captions, bold words, diagrams
- **Questions**: Passage-based MCQs, inference, vocabulary, text feature identification

## Module 2: Writing (25%)
- Narrative writing: plan a story with a beginning, middle, and end
- Persuasive writing: state an opinion and give two reasons
- Sentence structure: simple and compound sentences using "and", "but", "because"
- Paragraphing: group related ideas together
- Vocabulary: use precise words (not just "good" and "nice")
- **Questions**: Sentence improvement, paragraph ordering, writing prompts with rubric criteria

## Module 3: Language Conventions (25%)
- Spelling: common words, phonics patterns, homophones (there/their/they're)
- Grammar: nouns, verbs, adjectives, adverbs, pronouns
- Punctuation: capital letters, full stops, question marks, commas in lists, apostrophes for contractions (can't, don't)
- Sentence correction: find and fix errors
- **Questions**: Spelling MCQs, grammar fill-in, punctuation correction, error spotting

## Module 4: Numeracy (25%)
- Number and place value to 1000
- Addition and subtraction within 100 (mental and written)
- Multiplication: 2×, 5×, 10× tables
- Fractions: ½, ¼, ⅓ of shapes and quantities
- Measurement: length (cm, m), mass (g, kg), capacity (ml, l), time (5-min intervals)
- Data: read and interpret pictograms, bar charts, tables
- Geometry: 2D and 3D shape properties, symmetry
- **Questions**: Mixed numeracy MCQs mirroring NAPLAN item styles

## Stretch Zone
- Multi-step word problems
- Inference from longer passages (200+ words)
- Editing a full paragraph for spelling, grammar, and punctuation simultaneously
- Pattern and algebra: "What comes next in the sequence?"
- NAPLAN-style adaptive difficulty (harder questions unlock when earlier ones are correct)
