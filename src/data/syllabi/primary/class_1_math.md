# Class 1 Math
**ID**: `col-class-1-math` · **Discipline**: Maths · **Year**: 1 (ages 5–6)
**Frameworks**: UK KS1 National Curriculum, US Common Core Grade 1, ACARA v9 Year 1, India NCERT Class 1

## Module 1: Counting & Place Value (25%)
- Count to and across 100, forwards and backwards from any number
- Read and write numbers to 100 in numerals; to 20 in words
- Count in multiples of 2, 5, and 10
- Identify one more and one less than a given number
- Place value: understand tens and ones (e.g. 34 = 3 tens + 4 ones)
- Compare numbers using > < = symbols
- **Questions**: Fill-in sequences, "one more/one less", number-word matching, place value grids

## Module 2: Addition & Subtraction (25%)
- Add and subtract within 20 with fluency
- Number bonds to 10 and to 20
- Use related facts: if 7 + 3 = 10, then 10 − 3 = 7
- Solve one-step word problems (add to, take from, compare)
- Understand the equals sign as "the same as" (not just "the answer is")
- Missing number problems: 8 + ? = 13
- **Questions**: Mental arithmetic, word problems, number bond completion, missing addends

## Module 3: Measurement, Time & Data (25%)
- Compare and order lengths, weights, and capacities using direct comparison
- Measure using non-standard units (paperclips, cubes)
- Tell time to the hour and half past the hour (analogue and digital)
- Days of the week, months of the year in order
- Read and create simple pictograms and tally charts
- Recognise coins and notes (£/$/₹); make simple totals
- **Questions**: Clock reading, ordering by length, coin identification, tally chart interpretation

## Module 4: Geometry & Fractions (25%)
- Recognise and name 2D shapes: circle, triangle, square, rectangle, pentagon, hexagon
- Recognise and name 3D shapes: cube, cuboid, sphere, cylinder, cone, pyramid
- Describe position: left/right, top/bottom, above/below, between
- Describe movement: whole turn, half turn, quarter turn
- Recognise and find halves and quarters of shapes and small quantities
- Identify lines of symmetry in 2D shapes
- **Questions**: Shape identification, "half of 8", position descriptions, turn/rotation

## Stretch Zone
- Addition and subtraction within 30
- Introduction to number lines as a calculation tool
- Telling time to quarter past and quarter to
- Simple bar charts (beyond pictograms)
- Begin to understand odd and even numbers
