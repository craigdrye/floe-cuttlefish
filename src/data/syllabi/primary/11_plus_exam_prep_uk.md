# 11+ Exam Prep (UK)
**ID**: `11plusExamPrep` · **Discipline**: Exam Prep · **Year**: 6 (ages 10–11)
**Framework**: UK 11+ Grammar/Independent School Entrance Exams (GL Assessment, CEM, ISEB)

## Course Philosophy
This course is designed to prepare UK students for the highly competitive 11+ entrance examinations for grammar and independent schools. These exams test beyond the standard National Curriculum, requiring advanced reasoning skills, rapid mental calculation, and a broad vocabulary.

## Module 1: Verbal Reasoning (VR) (25%)
- **Vocabulary & Meaning**: Synonyms, antonyms, odd one out, word associations, homonyms
- **Alphabet & Word Codes**: Letter sequences, alphabet codes, number-to-word codes, finding hidden words
- **Word Manipulation**: Anagrams, compound words, inserting a letter to make a new word, completing words
- **Logical Deduction**: Solving logic puzzles from written clues (e.g., "If A is taller than B, and C is shorter than B...")
- **Questions**: Complex synonym/antonym matching, alphabet code cracking, logic puzzle deduction

## Module 2: Non-Verbal Reasoning (NVR) & Spatial Reasoning (25%)
- **Pattern Recognition**: Identifying the next shape in a sequence, finding the "odd one out" in a group of shapes
- **Analogies**: "Shape A is to Shape B as Shape C is to..."
- **Matrices**: Completing a 2x2 or 3x3 grid of shapes based on underlying rules
- **Spatial Reasoning**: 3D shapes, nets of cubes, rotations, reflections, symmetry, combining 2D shapes
- **Questions**: Sequence completion, matrix solving, identifying nets of cubes, rotation visualization

## Module 3: Advanced Mathematics (25%)
- **Mental Arithmetic**: Rapid calculation involving the four operations, fractions, decimals, and percentages
- **Multi-Step Problem Solving**: Complex word problems requiring multiple operations and logical steps
- **Number Properties**: Prime numbers, factors, multiples, square numbers, cube numbers, number sequences
- **Data & Geometry**: Interpreting complex charts/graphs, calculating mean/median/mode/range, calculating area/perimeter/volume of compound shapes, angle rules
- **Algebra**: Introductory algebra, solving simple equations, finding the *n*th term of a sequence
- **Questions**: Multi-step word problems, sequence finding (*n*th term), complex area/perimeter

## Module 4: English Comprehension & Grammar (25%)
- **Comprehension**: Reading complex, often classic/historical texts and answering questions involving inference, deduction, and understanding authorial intent
- **Vocabulary in Context**: Deducing the meaning of unfamiliar words from the text
- **Grammar & Syntax**: Identifying parts of speech (nouns, verbs, adjectives, adverbs, prepositions, conjunctions, pronouns)
- **Punctuation & Spelling**: Correcting errors in sentences, understanding complex spelling rules
- **Literary Devices**: Identifying and understanding similes, metaphors, personification, alliteration, onomatopoeia
- **Questions**: Inference-based comprehension, complex grammar identification, literary device analysis

## Stretch Zone
- Extremely tight time limits (simulating exam conditions where students often have less than 1 minute per question)
- Reading comprehension texts from 19th-century literature
- Multi-step logic puzzles involving 4+ variables
