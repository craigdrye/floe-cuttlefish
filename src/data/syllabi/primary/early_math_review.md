# Early Math Review
**ID**: `col-early-math-review` · **Discipline**: Maths · **Year**: 1 (ages 5–6)

## Course Philosophy
This is a consolidation course — it bridges the gap between pre-school maths and Year 1. Ideal for children who need to reinforce foundations before tackling Class 1 Math.

## Module 1: Number Confidence (30%)
- Secure counting 1–20 forwards and backwards
- Recognise all numerals 0–20
- Subitising to 5 (instant recognition without counting)
- One-to-one correspondence with objects
- Understand "none" / zero
- **Questions**: Count-and-match, numeral recognition, "how many?" from pictures

## Module 2: Simple Operations (30%)
- Addition within 10 using objects and pictures
- Subtraction within 10 as "taking away"
- Doubling small numbers (double 3 = 6)
- "One more" and "one less" up to 20
- Story problems: "You have 4 sweets. You eat 2. How many left?"
- **Questions**: Picture-based addition, subtraction stories, doubling

## Module 3: Patterns & Sorting (20%)
- Extend AB, ABB, ABC patterns with colours, shapes, numbers
- Sort objects by size, colour, shape, or type
- Identify the odd one out
- Copy and create simple patterns
- **Questions**: Pattern completion, sorting criteria identification

## Module 4: Shape & Space Basics (20%)
- Name circle, square, triangle, rectangle
- Find shapes in everyday objects
- Understand big/small, tall/short, long/short, heavy/light
- Simple positional language: in, on, under, next to
- **Questions**: Shape naming, comparison, spatial reasoning

## Stretch Zone
- Counting to 50
- Number bonds to 5 (fluent recall)
- Introduction to the number line
- Two-step patterns (e.g. +1, +2, +1, +2)
