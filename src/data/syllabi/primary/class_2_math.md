# Class 2 Math
**ID**: `col-class-2-math` · **Discipline**: Maths · **Year**: 2 (ages 6–7)
**Frameworks**: UK KS1 Year 2 National Curriculum, US Common Core Grade 2, ACARA v9 Year 2, India NCERT Class 2

## Module 1: Place Value & Number (25%)
- Read, write, and order numbers to at least 100 (stretch to 1000)
- Understand place value: hundreds, tens, ones
- Compare numbers using > < = with two- and three-digit numbers
- Count in steps of 2, 3, 5, and 10 from any number
- Partition two-digit numbers (e.g. 47 = 40 + 7)
- Estimate quantities and check by counting
- **Questions**: Ordering, partitioning, "what is the value of the 3 in 38?", counting sequences

## Module 2: Addition & Subtraction (25%)
- Add and subtract within 100 using mental and written strategies
- Column addition and subtraction (without regrouping, then with)
- Solve two-step word problems with + and −
- Inverse operations: use subtraction to check addition and vice versa
- Number bonds to 20 (fluent recall)
- Add three single-digit numbers
- **Questions**: Column sums, word problems, missing number equations, inverse checking

## Module 3: Multiplication & Division (25%)
- Recall 2×, 5×, and 10× tables (and related division facts)
- Understand multiplication as repeated addition and arrays
- Understand division as sharing equally and grouping
- Recognise odd and even numbers
- Solve simple multiplication and division word problems
- Commutativity of multiplication: 3 × 5 = 5 × 3
- **Questions**: Times table recall, array counting, "share 12 equally among 3", odd/even sorting

## Module 4: Fractions, Measurement & Geometry (25%)
- Recognise, find, name: ½, ⅓, ¼, ¾ of shapes, lengths, and quantities
- Equivalence: two quarters = one half
- Tell time to 5-minute intervals on analogue and digital clocks
- Measure length in cm and m, mass in g and kg, capacity in ml and l
- Recognise and describe properties of 2D and 3D shapes (edges, vertices, faces)
- Interpret simple pictograms, tally charts, and bar charts
- **Questions**: Fraction of a shape/quantity, clock reading, unit conversion, shape properties

## Stretch Zone
- Count in steps of 3 beyond the 3× table
- Add and subtract across 100 (e.g. 87 + 25)
- Introduction to the 3× and 4× tables
- Tell time to the nearest minute
- Roman numerals I to XII (clock faces)
- Simple line symmetry with more complex shapes
